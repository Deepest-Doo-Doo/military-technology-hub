F-22 Raptor

The F-22 Raptor is a fifth-generation stealth fighter designed to dominate the skies. It combines stealth technology, supercruise speed, and extreme agility to outperform nearly all other fighter aircraft.

Its stealth design allows it to avoid detection, while advanced avionics provide unmatched situational awareness. The F-22 can engage targets before being seen, giving it a massive advantage in modern air combat.

With thrust-vectoring engines, it can perform incredible maneuvers, making it highly effective in dogfights as well as long-range engagements.

Key Features

  • Stealth technology
  • Supercruise capability
  • Thrust vectoring maneuverability
  • Advanced avionics & sensors

Specifications

  • Max Speed: Mach 2+
  • Range: ~3,000 km
  • Engines: Twin Pratt & Whitney F119
  • Role: Air Superiority Fighter
